About Brain Jar

We're an independent game development studio focused on making games with character. Brain Jar began as a collaborative art project between two students, but the experience was so rewarding that the project continued beyond school.

Our game,Cosmic Trash, was featured at the Indie Games Festival in 2017. The team is currently working from their apartments, seperated by hundreds of miles and relying on the interent to create their games. As we learn and develop our skills, our games have grown in entertainment value and breadth of scope, but we try to remain true to the experimental and spontaneous nature of art making.

Several of our games are available free of charge. These represent our growth as game developers and serve as a promise of what's to come. We hope you enjoy our work and follow us to see what we create next.
