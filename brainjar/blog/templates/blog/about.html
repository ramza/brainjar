About Brain Jar

Brain Jar began as an art project and gateway into the world of game development for a couple of college students. Founded by artists Josh Petty and Russell White, Brain Jar has now created several video games and serves as a platform for the making of tutorials and other educational materials. We love making games and teaching others to make their own art.

Our game, Cosmic Trash, was featured at the Indie Games Festival in 2017.
